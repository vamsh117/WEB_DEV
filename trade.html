WHAT IS TRADE TOWER?

Trade Tower at Woxsen University is a dynamic center for nurturing entrepreneurship. It begins with energizing sensitization events, inspiring individuals to explore the startup ecosystem. For budding entrepreneurs with innovative ideas, Trade Tower offers a 12-month pre-incubation program, turning concepts into working prototypes with mentorship and resources. As startups develop prototypes, the 24-month incubation program provides comprehensive support to refine these into market-ready products. For startups ready to scale, the 4-6 month acceleration program focuses on rapid growth, offering intensive mentorship and resources. Trade Tower is a launchpad for transforming ideas into successful ventures, fostering innovation and entrepreneurial success at every stage.

Our Mission:

To be the premier destination and top choice for aspiring entrepreneurs, fostering innovation and supporting the creation of successful ventures.

Our Vision:

To build a disruptive startup ecosystem that jumpstarts the creation of unicorns.

Join us at Trade Tower and be part of a community where creativity meets opportunity and innovation lead to success.
