Trade Tower has various programs that support startups at different levels.The programs are categorized into three parts depending on the stage of the startup.

Pre-Incubation

Trade Tower’s pre-incubation program is designed to offer aspiring entrepreneurs a robust foundation for turning their innovative ideas into successful prototypes.The pre-incubation program at Trade Tower typically spans 12 months with a focus on mentorship, resources, and hands-on support, participants are well-equipped to navigate the early stages of their entrepreneurial journey and prepare for future growth.

Eligibility

The program is open to idea-stage entrepreneurs who have a clear vision and a commitment to developing their startup concept.

Apply Now

Incubation

Trade Tower’s incubation program is tailored for startups that have moved beyond the idea stage and are ready to scale their operations. The incubation program at Trade Tower typically spans 12 to 24 months, depending on the needs and progress of each startup. The goal is to accelerate startups towards market readiness and sustainable success.

Eligibility

The program is designed for early-stage startups that have a viable product or prototype and are seeking to scale their operations and achieve significant growth.

Apply Now

Short-Term Training Program

Trade Tower's short-term training program is designed to offer focused, intensive learning experiences for individuals in a condensed time frame. The short-term training programs at Trade Tower are designed to be completed in a brief period, typically ranging from a few days to several weeks, depending on the specific course and its depth.

Eligibility

These programs are open to individuals, professionals, and businesses interested in enhancing their skills and knowledge in particular areas. No prior experience is usually required, though some programs may have prerequisites.

Apply Now
