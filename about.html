About us

What We Do

"World's first University Incubator with the mission to jumpstart future unicorns"

Trade Tower affiliated with Woxsen University is a vibrant hub of innovation, entrepreneurship and skill development. It is designed to foster a disruptive culture that propels the creation of impactful startups. We stand apart from the market players by embracing an unconventional yet powerful strategy to accelerate the development of great ventures.

At Trade Tower, we believe in doing things differently. Our approach combines cutting-edge resources, expert mentorship, and a dynamic network to support aspiring entrepreneurs at every stage of their journey. By challenging norms and fostering innovative thinking, we aim to nurture the next generation of leaders and innovators who drive economic growth and societal impact.

How Does it Work?

1

Innovation Lab

  • Idea generation, refinement, development of customer centric prototype
  • Technology mapping
2

Business Analysis

  • Develop viable B-plans with advice from faculty and domain experts
  • Strategy simulations to understand scope of market strategies
3

Business Advisory

  • Refine B-plans and strategies with help from mentors
  • Build valuable business networks
  • Personality assessment and refinement
4

Commercialisation

  • Technology commercialisation and IP management
  • Identify strategic partners (if required)
  • Management team consolidation and incorporation
5

Funding

  • Pitch business plan to members of the Woxsen fund
  • Access to funding from other sources
  • Establish legal frameworks and incorporate firm
